Frequently Asked Questions

How should I prepare the space for a shoot?

I'll send you a detailed prep guide, but generally: declutter surfaces, remove personal items, and ensure all bulbs are working. I'll handle the rest — adjusting furniture, styling vignettes, and optimizing the space for camera.

Do you work directly with my clients?

Absolutely. I can coordinate directly with homeowners or work through you — whatever makes the project smoother. I'm professional and discreet.

How long does a typical shoot take?

A standard residential interior shoot takes 2-4 hours depending on square footage and complexity. Larger projects or multiple spaces may require a full day.

Can I use the photos anywhere?

Yes — you receive full commercial usage rights. Use them on your website, social media, in publications, for awards, and in client presentations.

What about staging? Should I hire a stager?

If the space is already designed and furnished, usually no additional staging is needed. I can advise during our pre-shoot call if I think anything would help.

Do you photograph commercial interiors too?

Yes — offices, retail spaces, restaurants, hospitality, and more. The approach is similar, with attention to brand and functionality.
